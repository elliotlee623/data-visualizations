Win Streaks in Illini Football

We explored every football game from 1892 through 2015, calculating the win streak for the Illini verses every team they've played.

Every circle represents an Illini football game. A blue circle represents a win; a red circle a loss. The larger the circle, the greater the Illini win streak coming into the game.
